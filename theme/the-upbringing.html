The Seattle Sounders were introduced into the MLS (Major League Soccer) in 2009 where they took the league by storm after beating the New York Redbulls three to zero on their opening game. Before the Seattle Sounders FC were able to Be one of the best teams in the league they had to get the money to make the team. With that we come to Paul Allen the previous owner of the Seattle Sounders before his death. Paul Allen owned the Seattle Sounders along with Joe Roth.

The Seattle Sounders already were dominant in their past years and leagues and Seattle was a hotspot for sports teams already since it is so populated. The Sounders were able to come to Seattle since their stadium was made for international soccer games, normal soccer games and football.
